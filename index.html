Learn more about Palomino

Palomino is a peaceful coastal village on Colombia’s Caribbean coast. Known for its palm-lined beaches, laid-back atmosphere, and scenic river tubing, it’s the perfect spot to relax, surf, or explore nature between the ocean and the jungle.

Learn more about Santa Marta

Santa Marta is a vibrant coastal city and one of Colombia’s oldest. It offers easy access to Tayrona National Park, where lush jungle meets crystal-clear Caribbean beaches — perfect for hiking, relaxing, and experiencing nature at its best.

Learn more about Salento

Salento is a charming town in Colombia’s coffee region, surrounded by green hills and famous for its colorful streets and artisan shops. It’s the gateway to the stunning Cocora Valley, home to the tallest palm trees in the world.
